Monteverde market hall

Master thesis project.
Market hall in Monteverde district in Rome.
The building is not only a market but also a new public space for the inhabitants of the neighbourhood.
The project is based on the concept of sustainability in all its aspects, both environmental and social and economic.

Galgano abbey museum

Project competition.
New museum for San Galgano Abbey.
The project establishes a dialogue between past and present, between the contemporary museum and the historical pre-existence.
The design of the new volume is generated by the existing brick wall of the ancient cloister.

Apertamente

University project at TU Wien.
Masterplan and housing project for the district of Gretta in Triest.
In addition to the housing blocks, the project includes co-working spaces, a youth center and a market.

Studio per la Citta'

University project in collaboration with MAXXI Museum in Rome.
Exhibition about works of the architect Michele Valori.

Feuermauer

University project at TU Wien.
A project of micro-urbanism.
The project transforms the fire walls, the blind facades, into temporary works of art.
